FAQ’s

Common Asked Questions

UpList is a next-generation C2C (consumer-to-consumer) marketplace where individuals can buy and sell quality pre-owned and new goods. Powered by India’s ONDC network, UpList ensures secure transactions, verified sellers, and faster logistics.

It’s super easy. If you’re selling, just snap a photo, list your item, and wait for buyers to reach out. If you’re buying, browse what you need, chat with the seller, and place your order. We handle the delivery and payments through our trusted ONDC partners.

Nope, listing your items is completely free. We only take a small fee after a successful transaction to help keep the platform running smoothly and securely.

You can sell almost anything in good condition. Popular items include smartphones, laptops, TVs, books, clothes, bikes, cars, furniture, home appliances, and more. As long as it’s legal and useful, someone’s probably looking for it.

Every user has a profile with ratings, reviews, and a trust score. We also verify identities and monitor activity to help you buy and sell with confidence.

Once your item is sold, we arrange for a reliable pickup and delivery using ONDC-enabled logistics partners. In many areas, deliveries happen within 24 hours or even on the same day.

UpList is built with modern users in mind. It’s cleaner, faster, and designed around trust. We don’t just connect people — we also provide verified profiles, secure payments, fast deliveries, and responsive customer support.

If you come across a suspicious item or seller, tap the “Report” button on the listing. Our moderation team will investigate and take appropriate action.

We accept all major UPI apps, debit/credit cards, and net banking through ONDC’s secure payment gateway. Cash on delivery may be available in select areas.

You can reach our support team via the Help Center in the app or website, or email us at info@uplist.co.in We’re available 7 days a week to assist you.
